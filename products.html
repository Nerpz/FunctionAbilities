JACO Assistive Robotic Arm

Featuring a two or three finger hand thats capable of picking objects off the floor, assisting with eating or drinking and much more, the JACO can improve quality of life for nearly everyone.

The arm can be mounted to almost all powered wheelchairs and is controlled with the wheelchairs existing joystick, head array system, or other controls. Made of lightweight carbon fiber, the arm is efficient and durable. Being so lightweight, the JACO is efficient when powered with the wheelchairs existing battery supply. The JACO Assistive Robotic Arm is the preferred option by many clients.

WREX

The WREX is a modular body-powered orthosis that is mounted directly to a persons wheelchair.

It is a two-segment, four-degrees-of-freedom exoskeletal arm, energized by elastic bands. The WREX allows full passive range of motion for the arm and provides a sense of flotation that assists in voluntary movement. It can easily be adjusted to accommodate subjects of different size, weight, and arm lengths by changing the number of bands or sliding the telescoping links.

Multi-Link

The Multi-Link allows significantly greater range of motion for eating, drinking and facial hygiene. This design has more lifting power with fewer rubber bands.
